About Me

My name is Yusuf Nashif and I am a Senior in Information Science. I am a motivated student hoping to make a difference in the world by educating individuals about the importance of privacy and cybersecurity. Through my studies and experiences, I have gained skills in IT, cybersecurity, and digital problem-solving, which I aim to apply in meaningful ways. Currently, I have an internship with TechSmith, where I work as a Customer Care intern. My dream is to one day own a business that focuses on creating and providing necessary and effective anti-virus software that protects vulnerable individuals from harmful attacks online.

Outside of my work, I enjoy playing video games, listening to music, baking, rock-climbing, and I find excitement in trying new things! I enjoy rock climbing because it combines physical activity with strategic problem-solving by pushing me to think carefully about each move while utilizing my strength and endurance. Listening to music and baking are hobbies that I find therapeutic, and I often rely on them when I need to relieve stress in a less physically demanding form.
